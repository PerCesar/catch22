Bird Migration in New York City

Spring and Fall Migration in New York City New York City is situated directly in the Atlantic Flyway, the migration corridor used by birds in the spring and fall seasons. Here, NYC Parks & Recreation lists common migratory birds and where they can be found, such as the piping plovers that nest in Rockaway Beach each spring. You can also find tips on keeping migratory birds safe during their stay, like making your windows bird-safe and reporting any injured birds.
